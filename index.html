Note the developer

If you've made it this far and you like reading words, I'd like talk a little about the game.
Stir Party has been a passion project for me for since June 2017. When Steam opened up their marketplace, I knew I wanted to self publish a game. I landed on doing a party game when I realized I wanted to make a multiplayer game, and I couldn't tie myself down to one idea. Having multiple modes freed me to implement whatever kind of gamemode I dreamed up, and it's been a blast. Over the past three years I've grown as a developer, artist, and person.
However well Stir Party does, I just wanted to do it, and actually reaching people was an after thought. The fact you're even reading this means everything to me.
Thank you.
